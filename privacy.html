Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This website (anatganot.com) is the personal portfolio of Anat Ganot. I care about your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum. This page explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information I collect

When you contact me. If you email, call, or message me on WhatsApp using the links on this site, I receive the contact details and any information you choose to share.

When you book a call. The "Virtual Cafe" booking uses Google Calendar Appointment Scheduling. When you book, the name, email, and any details you enter are processed by Google and added to my Google Calendar so we can meet.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use analytics or advertising cookies, and its fonts are hosted on the site itself (no third-party font tracking). The only third-party element that may set cookies is the Google booking calendar on the Virtual Cafe page. It loads only after you accept via the cookie notice. If you decline, the calendar isn't loaded, and you can still reach me by email or WhatsApp.

Third-party services

Google (Calendar / Workspace) — for scheduling and email. GitHub Pages — hosts this website; like most web hosts, it may log visitors' IP addresses for security and reliability.

How I use your information

I use your information only to respond to you and to schedule and hold our conversation or work together. I do not sell your information or share it for marketing.

Keeping and deleting your information

I keep your information only as long as needed to communicate or work with you. You can ask me to access or delete the information I hold about you at any time — just email me.

Contact
